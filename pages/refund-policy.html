Effective Date: June 27, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 27, 2026

This Refund and Cancellation Policy applies to all courses and services purchased through Capital Finplus Academy ("CFA", "we", "us", or "our") at capitalfinplusadvizors.com. Please read this policy carefully before making any purchase.

No Refund Policy

All purchases made on Capital Finplus Academy are final and non-refundable. Once a course or service is purchased, no refunds, exchanges, or credits will be issued under any circumstances.

1. Why We Have a No-Refund Policy

Digital educational content is delivered and accessible immediately upon purchase. Because our course material — including videos, frameworks, methodologies, and proprietary trading systems — is instantly accessible once payment is confirmed, we are unable to "un-deliver" the content. Therefore, all sales are final.

2. Before You Purchase

We strongly encourage all prospective students to take advantage of our resources before making a purchase decision:

  • Free Discovery Call: Book a no-obligation 30-minute 1-on-1 session with our mentor to understand if our courses are right for you
  • Course Preview: Every course's "What's Included" popup gives you the full curriculum breakdown and a preview of the material before you buy
  • Free Articles & Backtests: Browse our free blog articles and recent backtests to experience our teaching style and content quality
  • Contact Us: Email us at connect@capitalfinplusadvizors.com with any questions before purchasing

3. What Is Covered

Our no-refund policy applies to all products and services including:

  • All paid courses (e.g., Guided Learning, Mentorship Program)
  • 1-on-1 mentorship sessions
  • Live trading session access
  • Course bundles and packages
  • Any future paid offerings on this platform

4. Cancellations

Once a purchase is completed, it cannot be cancelled. If you change your mind after purchase, we cannot process a cancellation or issue a refund. We recommend you complete your due diligence — using our free resources and Discovery Call — before purchasing.

5. Technical Issues & Access Problems

If you experience technical difficulties accessing your purchased course, please contact us immediately at connect@capitalfinplusadvizors.com. We will work to resolve any genuine technical access issues promptly. Technical difficulties do not qualify for refunds but will be resolved by our team.

If you face any access issues after purchase, email us within 7 days at connect@capitalfinplusadvizors.com and we will resolve it within 48 hours.

6. Duplicate Payments

In the rare event of a duplicate payment (i.e., you are charged twice for the same order due to a technical error), please contact us at connect@capitalfinplusadvizors.com with your payment receipt(s). Verified duplicate charges will be refunded to the original payment method within 7–10 business days.

Initiating a chargeback with your bank or payment provider without first contacting us is a violation of these Terms. If a chargeback is initiated, we reserve the right to permanently revoke your access to all purchased content and take further legal action if necessary.
